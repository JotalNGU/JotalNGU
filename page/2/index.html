英语学习---翻译常见句型、技巧（十）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

  1. China is a country with a time-honored civilization and also a land of ceremony and decorum.

    中国是一个文化历史悠久的国度，也是一个礼仪之邦。

    解析：

    1. time-honored 历史悠久的
    2. ceremony and decorum 礼仪

  2. Whenever guests visit, it is necessary to make and serve tea to them.

    每当客人来访，都需要泡茶给客人喝。

    解析：

    1. guest 客人
    2. it is necessary to 必须的
    3. make and serve tea 泡茶

  3. Usually, if the tea is made in a teacup, boiling water should be added into the cup when half of the tea in it has been consumed;

    通常，若是用茶杯泡的茶，在茶喝完一半之后就应该加开水

    解析：

    1. boiling water should be added into （要多用被动语态）
    2. characteristic 特点
    3. consume 消耗

  4. Before serving tea, you may ask them for their preferences as to what kind of tea they fancy, and serve them the tea in the most appropriate teacups.

    在给客人奉茶之前，你应该问问他们都喜欢喝什么类型的茶，并采用最合适的茶具奉上。

    解析：

    1. before doing 在做什么之前
    2. after doing 在做什么之后
    3. preferences喜好
    4. appropriate 适合的

    在线教育，也被称为远程教育，是指通过应用信息技术和互联网技术进行内容传播和快速学习的教育模式。

    解析：

    1. also known as 也被称为
    2. distance learning 远程教育
    3. ”是指通过应用信息技术和互联网技术进行内容传播和快速学习的教育模式“ 中“通过应用信息技术和互联网技术进行内容传播和快速学习的”是教育模式的定语， 定语过长应该后置。

    5. technology 技术

  2. Compared to the traditional education, it can break the limits of time and space, and make the places of learning and channels of acquiring knowledge flexible.

    与传统教育相比，它可以打破时空的限制，使学习的场所和获取知识的渠道灵活多变

    解析：

    1. compared to 与 。。对比
    2. time and space 时空（时间和空间）
    3. the limit of 。。。的限制
    4. channel 渠道、频道
    5. acquire knowledge 获取知识
    6. flexible 灵活的

  3. Based on its characteristics and advantages, e-learning gradually enters people’s life and becomes a main trend of learning.

    基于它的特点和优势，在线教育逐渐走进大众的生活，成为了一种学习的主流趋势。

    解析：

    1. based on 基于
    2. characteristic 特点
    3. main trend 主流

英语学习---翻译常见句型、技巧（五）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

1.Qixi，known as Chinese Valentine’s Day, falls on the seventh day of the seventh lunar month.

每年农历七月初七，即七夕，是中国的情人节。

解析：

  1. be known as 被公认为… , 这里没有be ，因为后面的falls 作谓语，前面的是做同位语。记住一个句子只有一个谓语
  2. falls on 表示在哪一个日期
  3. lunar 农历

    2.It is believed that you can see Niulang and Zhinv dating across the Milky Way if you look up at the sky on the evening of Qixi.

传说如果你在七夕那晚抬头看天空就能看到牛郎和织女在银河约会。

解析：

  1. It is believed that 传说
  2. see sb doing 看见….正在…
  3. on the evening of Qixi 在具体一天晚上 用on，例如 on Saturday night
    • 泛指用in、at ： in the evening at night

3.Qixi not only embodies people’s yearning and pursuit for ideal love, but also inherits and promotes the fine traditional virtues of the Chinese nation.

七夕不仅体现了人们对理想爱情的向往和追求，并且传承和发扬了中华民族优良的传统美德。

解析：

  1. not only… but also 不仅而且
  2. embody 代表
  3. yearning 向往
  4. pursuit 追求
  5. inherit 传承
  6. promote 发扬
  7. fine traditional virtues 传统美德

英语学习---翻译常见句型、技巧（二）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

1.Currently, being one of the largest exporters in the world, China is attracting massive foreign investment。

目前，中国是最大的出口国之一，并正在吸引大量的外国投资。

解析：

  1. being one of the largest exporters in the world 。
    • 采用非谓语结构，避免单调和重复；
    • one of the…名词是复数
  2. export 出口 exporter 出口国 import 进口
  3. massive 大量的
  4. attract 吸引
  5. investment 投资n. invest 投资v.

2.In 2011, China surpassed Japan, becoming the second largest economic entity in the world。

中国在2011年超过日本成为世界上第二大经济体。

解析：

  1. surpass 超过，此处是过去式加ed
  2. second largest 第二大的 third longest 第三长的 ，序数词+形容词最高级

3.Many elements that form the foundation of the modern world originate from China.

构成现代世界基础的许多元素都起源于中国。

解析：

  1. 句子主干是：元素起源于中国。所以，“构成现代世界基础的” 用定语从句修饰先行词”元素“element.
  2. form the foundation of … 构成…的基础
  3. originate from 起源于 v

  1. Crosstalk is a traditional Chinese comedic performance in the form of dialogue.

    相声是一种以对话为形式的中国传统戏剧表演

    解析：

    1. performance 表演
    2. in the form of … 以。。。为形式
    3. dialogue 对话

  2. Crosstalk is an art of language, as well as an art of humor.

    相声是语言的艺术，也是幽默的艺术。

    2. an art of 。。。 什么的艺术

  3. However，these two features are not isolated, but are interdependent.

    但这两大特点不是孤立的，而是相互依存的。

    解析：

    1. not 。。。but 不是而是
    2. isolated 孤立的
    3. interdependent 相互依存的

  4. The crosstalk is made up of four fundamental skills–speaking,imitating,teasing and singings, and aims to entertain the audience by using humorous words and dialogues.

    相声的四大基本功是说学逗唱，其目的是通过幽默的话语达到娱乐观众的效果。

英语学习---翻译常见句型、技巧（一）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

1.云南省的丽江古镇是中国著名的旅游目的地之一。

Lijiang，an ancient town in Yunnan Province, is one of the most famous tourist attractions in China.

解析：

  1. Lijiang，an ancient town in Yunnan Province, 主语是Lijiang，云南省的古镇做同位语，使句子简洁。
  2. one of the most famous 常用表达：最什么之一
  3. 常用短语：tourist attractions 或 travel destinations

2.丽江还以“爱之城”而闻名。

It has also been known as the “City of Love” in his history.

解析：

  1. 常用短语：be famous/known as(以。。。闻名) as表作为
  2. be famous/known for(因。。。而闻名)for表因为

3.如今，在中外游客眼中，丽江被视为爱情和浪漫的天堂。

Nowadays, this ancient town is regarded as the paradise of love and romance in the eyes of both Chinese and foreign visitors.

解析：

  1. 常用短语：be regarded as 被视为
  2. in the eyes of 在、、眼中
  3. both Chinese and foreign visitors 中外游客

英语学习---作文模板（问题解决型）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

问题解决型作文写作思路：

第一段：引出问题

第二段：分析问题出现的原因

第三段：问题导致的后果，解决措施

首段

  1. Along with the advance of the society more and more problems are brought to our attention, one of which is that + 问题 （描述出现的问题）

  2. Undoubtedly, we must be aware of the great harmfulness of this problem. （阐述问题的危害性）

    ​

中间段

  1. There are numerous reasons accounting for this problem, and I would like to explore a few of the most important ones here. To begin with…..In the second place……The last but not least……

    分析出现问题的原因

尾段

  1. There is no doubt the issue has exerted harmful effects on+危害 （问题导致的后果/危害）

通过以上的讨论，我们可以得出如下结论，___。

  1. Therefore，some measures should be taken to this problem. (引出策略)

  2. 列举策略…Only in this way can we …. (写策略)

例文

Along with the advance of the society more and more problems are brought to our attention, one of which is that the world we live in is becoming increasingly intolerable. The environment has suffered serious damage. Undoubtedly, we must be aware of the great harmfulness of this problem.

There are numerous reasons accounting for this problem, and I would like to explore a few of the most important ones here. To begin with, with the development of industrialization, more and more resources are needed. Human beings exploit resources recklessly. In the second place, being used vehicle by people, a lot of poisonous gases are emitted, which contribute to greenhouse effect. The last but not least, the use of chemical substances in agriculture also causes environmental pollution.

There is no doubt the issue has exerted harmful effects on the healthy of our human beings. Therefore，some measures should be taken to this problem. In the first place, Our natural resources will last longer if we learn to recycle them. What’s more, we can stop driving personal cars. Only in these way can we, have a better and cleaner planet in the future.
