英语学习---作文模板（应用文——书信）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

书信作文写作思路：

书信主要是注意格式，没有固定的模板，要写什么都是根据题目来。所以下面给出一些常见的书信主题的类型。

申请信(求职)

Dear …,

I am extremely pleased to see your advertisement for the position in ….And I would like to write a letter to tell you that I am suitable for the kind of the job you are advertising.

我非常高兴看到您招聘…职位的广告，我写信想告诉你的是我适合你们的那份工作。

I feel I am competent to meet the requirements you have listed.On the one hand, …. On the other hand, …. （这里说自己有什么能力，适合职位的需要等等，尽量多扯一点）I am enclosing my resume for your kind consideration and reference.

我觉得有能力满足你所列举的要求。一方面。。。另一方面。。。我在此附上我的简历供您参考和考虑。

I shall be much obliged if you will offer me a precious opportunity to an interview.I am looking forward to your replies at your earliest convenience.

我会很感激如果你给我一个面试机会。我希望如果你方便请尽早回复。

Best regards for your health and success.

​ Sincerely yours，

​ xxx

感谢信

　　Dear_________,

　　I am writing this letter to express my sincere gratitude for___________.Had it not been for your assistance in ___,I fear that ___may not have____________.I’d like you to know that your help __.It not only____________,but also__________.I shall always remember__________.

我写这封信想表达真诚的感激因为…. 如果没有你帮助我做… 我恐怕不能…. 我想让你知道你的帮助对我很重要，不仅。。而且… 我会一直记住你的帮助的。

　　I hope that I will have the opportunity of returning your hospitality by inviting you to __.Will you kindly let me know whether you____________?I am looking forward to seeing you soon!

我希望我有机会通过邀请你做…以回报你的好意 。我想知道你是否愿意接受？ 我想快点看到你。

　　Again,I would like to express my thanks to .Please accept my appreciation and pass my best wishes on to ____.I hope to hear from you soon.

再一次表达我的感谢。请接收我的感激，并帮我向…传达敬意。希望快点收到你的回信、

　　 Sincerely yours,

　 　xxx

慰问信

Dear___________,

　　I read from today’s newspaper about your accident, and I am writing to extend my deepest condolences.

我从今天的报纸得知你的意外（要具体说）,我写信想表达我最深的同情。

　　It broke my heart to hear that.I could not believe my eyes ,and I am really worried about you and your family. I am crossing my fingers for you now in hope that________.

当我得知的时候我伤碎了心。我不能相信我的眼睛,我很担心你和你的家人。我交叉手指希望你….

　　I can imagine how difficult it must be for you , and I want to let you know that you have my full support.Please let me know if I can help you with anything else.

我能想象你当时肯定很困难，我想让你知道我会全力保护你。请你告你我如果你需要其他什么帮助。

　　I feel extremely sorry for the accident,and I am convinced that you will overcome the difficulty and embrace a better life.

我对这次的意外感到很对不起，但我肯定你会客服这次困难然后拥抱一个更好的生活。

　 　Sincere yours,

　 　xxx

英语学习---作文模板（图表类）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

图表类作文写作思路：

图表主要内容包括文字和图表，两者都很重要。图表的类型主要有：柱状图，饼图，折线图，统计表格等。

第一段：描述总体趋势，再挑一个明显变化的具体描述，分析图表的含义

第二段：分析图表出现这种趋势的原因

第三段：总结，自己的观点，前景预测等。

首段

  1. As can be seen from the chart/ table, it displays the changes of…from …to..

    Especially, from xx to xx has witnessed dramatic changes in… While … It is obvious from the table that…

    正如图表/表格中所见，他展示了从。。到。。时间的什么的变化。特别的是，在xx到xx时间有了引人注目的变化 ，同时….. 从图表明显可以知道+内涵

中间段

  1. There are numerous reasons accounting for the changes, and I would like to explore a few of the most important ones here. To begin with…..In the second place……The last but not least……

尾段

  1. From what has been discussed above, we may finally draw the conclusion that + 总结

  2. Moreover, it seems that this trend will last in the foreseeable future.

    另外，看起来这种趋势会在可预见的未来持续。

例文

How People Spent Their Holidays

Year 1990 1995 2000
Traveling 37% 51% 76%
Staying at home 63% 49% 24%

As can be seen from the table, it displays the changes of ways of people spent their holidays. Especially, from 1995 to 2000 has witnessed dramatic changes. In 1990, 63% of people spent their holidays at home, while the figure dropped considerably to 24% in 2000. It is obvious from the table that people tended to be more and more mobile, and they wanted to get entertainment in the outside world.

　　There are numerous reasons accounting for the changes, and I would like to explore a few of the most important ones here. To begin with, with the rapid development of economy, people earn by far more money than they used to. Thus, people are able to afford traveling expenses. In the second place, people think it important to get well-informed about the outside world in this new era when everything is on the move. Traveling, of course, is a sure way to achieve this goal. What’s more, in this competitive society, people are usually under great stress. However, it is not difficult to find traveling an effective way to relieve people’s stress and get relaxation. Last but not least, by traveling outside, people are close to nature, which is not only beneficial to both their health and peace of mind.

From what has been discussed above, we may finally draw the conclusion that people prefer to go traveling rather than stay at home during holidays for a combination of reasons. Moreover, it seems that this trend will last in the foreseeable future.

英语学习---作文模板（名言类）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

名言类作文写作思路：

第一段：引出谚语，谚语的内涵

第二段：具体举例说明（或者说xx的重要性）

第三段：总结, 说一下自己对这句名言的看法，以及呼吁人们应该学习这句名言。

首段

  1. It is often said that+ 名言. What the saying is trying to indicate is that+ 内涵。Indeed, we can learn many things from it.

    有一句名言经常被说起….. 这句名言表明的是…… 实际上，我们可以从这句话学到很多东西。

中间段

  1. 两种写法：

    • 一种名言是可以扯到重要性的：就列举它的重要性 There are numerous factors, The first and most important reason is that…There is another factor that deserves some words here,…. The last but not least…..

    • 平常的，不能扯到重要性的，就吹一些当今社会与名言相关话，然后说一两个实际例子。Our society abounds with the example of the proverb. + 举例说明

尾段

  1. From what has been discussed above, we may finally draw the conclusion that + 名言对我们的好处。From my point of view, only through +名言具体的精神+can we achieve our potential and live a better life in this rapidly developing society.(呼吁)

例文

It is often said that learning is a daily experience and a lifetime mission. What the saying is trying to indicate is that lifelong learning is essential. Indeed, we can learn many things from it.

Lifelong learning is crucial to our life and career in modern society. Living in the Information Age, We have to keep pace with times through ceaseless learning; Otherwise, we will be too outdated to seize any opportunities. For example, a taxi driver who learns to use a Taxi App such as Didi can make much more money than those who do not; A housewife who opens a shop online can even out-earn a white-collar worker.

From what has been discussed above, we may finally draw the conclusion that the lifelong learning is important. From my point of view, only through learning ceaselessly can we achieve our potential and live a better life in this rapidly developing society.

前言

在数据结构中，线性表是一种简单而常用的数据结构，也是其他复杂数据逻辑结构的基础。线性表中数据元素是“一个接一个”依次排序的，排在第一的数据元素称为表头，最后的数据元素称为表尾，在某个元素的前一个称为该元素的直接前驱，后一个称为直接后继。对于非空线性表来说，表头是唯一一个没有直接前驱的数据元素，表尾是唯一一个没有直接后继的元素。同一个线性表中，数据元素需要同一个数据类型。
