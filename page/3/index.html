英语学习---作文模板（问题解决型）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

问题解决型作文写作思路：

第一段：引出问题

第二段：分析问题出现的原因

第三段：问题导致的后果，解决措施

首段

  1. Along with the advance of the society more and more problems are brought to our attention, one of which is that + 问题 （描述出现的问题）

  2. Undoubtedly, we must be aware of the great harmfulness of this problem. （阐述问题的危害性）

    ​

中间段

  1. There are numerous reasons accounting for this problem, and I would like to explore a few of the most important ones here. To begin with…..In the second place……The last but not least……

    分析出现问题的原因

尾段

  1. There is no doubt the issue has exerted harmful effects on+危害 （问题导致的后果/危害）

通过以上的讨论，我们可以得出如下结论，___。

  1. Therefore，some measures should be taken to this problem. (引出策略)

  2. 列举策略…Only in this way can we …. (写策略)

例文

Along with the advance of the society more and more problems are brought to our attention, one of which is that the world we live in is becoming increasingly intolerable. The environment has suffered serious damage. Undoubtedly, we must be aware of the great harmfulness of this problem.

There are numerous reasons accounting for this problem, and I would like to explore a few of the most important ones here. To begin with, with the development of industrialization, more and more resources are needed. Human beings exploit resources recklessly. In the second place, being used vehicle by people, a lot of poisonous gases are emitted, which contribute to greenhouse effect. The last but not least, the use of chemical substances in agriculture also causes environmental pollution.

There is no doubt the issue has exerted harmful effects on the healthy of our human beings. Therefore，some measures should be taken to this problem. In the first place, Our natural resources will last longer if we learn to recycle them. What’s more, we can stop driving personal cars. Only in these way can we, have a better and cleaner planet in the future.

英语学习---作文模板（观点对比型）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

观点对比型作文写作思路：

第一段：描述论点

第二段：分别列出两种观点和理由

第三段：自己的观点和理由

首段

  1. There is a public controversy nowadays over the issue of + 论点. People
    from different backgrounds hold different attitudes towards the issue.

    最近，关于……是一个公共的争论。来自不同背景的人对这个话题有不同的看法。

中间段

  1. Some people hold the idea that + 观点. In their point of view, 列举一到两个他们的理由. However, some people set completely different argument concerning
    this case. They believe that + 观点. One of the most important reasons is that+ 说一个理由。

    一些人持……观点，在他们的观点中，+理由；然而，另一些人考虑这个话题的时候持有完全不同的观点。他们认为……，其中一个重要的原因是……

尾段

  1. From my perspective, I am in favor of the former/latter point of view. There are numerous reasons accounting for my standpoint, and I would like to explore a few of the most important ones here. To begin with…..In the second place……The last but not least……

    在我的观点中，我更加支持前者/后者的观点。有很多理由支持着我的观点，我将在这里列举一些重要的。……

    或者说 the two standpoints have their roles respectively in this controversy.（都有可取之处），然后说自己的理由

例文

Should college students be decompressed or increased pressure?

大学生应该减压还是增压？

There is a public controversy nowadays over the issue of college students decompress or increase pressure. People from different backgrounds hold different attitudes towards the issue.

Some people hold the idea that college students supposed to(应该) decompress. In their point of view, nowadays, college students are under great pressure from such as graduation to find jobs. However, some people set completely different argument concerning this case. They believe that college students should be increased pressure. One of the most important reasons is that Nowadays, many college students do not try to improve their abilities in universities. As a result, they could not have a good perspective.

From my perspective, I am in favor of the latter point of view. In order to enhance students’ individual competitiveness, The university should increase pressure to college students. Only in this way can us college student have a good future and protect our motherland.

英语学习---作文模板（漫画作文）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

漫画作文写作思路：

第一段描述图片内容，引出内涵/现象。

第二段解析原因，论证观点等。

第三段总结结论，提出建议，展望未来等。

首段

  1. It is vividly depicted in the picture that+图画中的人物、话语或事件 （描述画面）
  2. What the picture is trying to present is that + 内涵/现象 （引出内涵）
  3. 第三句有两种写法：
    • However, from my perspective, +自己的观点
    • There is no doubt that this phenomenon is a dangerous signal for our society. 说明现象的影响（一般是消极的）

中间段

  1. There are numerous reasons accounting for ____, and I would like to explore a few of the most important ones here. To begin with…..In the second place……Last but not least……

    横线处：如果是现象就写this phenomenon， 然后在分析原因。如果是观点就写my standpoint, 然后论证自己的观点。

尾段

  1. From what has been discussed above, we may finally draw the conclusion that… （重申现象的影响或者自己的观点）

通过以上的讨论，我们可以得出如下结论，___。

  1. There is no doubt that enough concern must be paid to this problem. (引出策略)
  2. Therefore，it is high time we… Only in this way can we …. (写策略)

例文

Directions: For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write a short essay based on the picture below. You should focus on the impact of social networking websites on reading. You are required to write at least 150 words but no more than 200 words.

It is vividly depicted in the picture in which two lovers are discussing about their hobby of reading. The guy tells his girlfriend, “I love reading. I read about 3 hours a day. My favorite book is Face book”.What the picture present is that the social networking websites has a great impact on reading.

There are numerous reasons accounting for this standpoint, and I would like to explore a few of the most important ones here. To begin with, it is a common phenomenon for a host of youngsters to spend too much time in reading, sharing and chatting with friends on social networks. It is these websites that do not enable teenagers to have adequate opportunities or time to read traditional books. In the second place, as the social networking websites, they have brought us a lots of information.However, some young people have become addicted to these social websites.

From what has been discussed above, we may finally draw the conclusion that the social networking websites has a great impact on reading.There is no doubt that enough concern must be paid to this problem. Therefore，it is high time we turn our focus to traditional books, like classic novels, poems and essays. Only in this way can we acquire the real knowledge.

英语学习---作文模板（图表类）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

图表类作文写作思路：

图表主要内容包括文字和图表，两者都很重要。图表的类型主要有：柱状图，饼图，折线图，统计表格等。

第一段：描述总体趋势，再挑一个明显变化的具体描述，分析图表的含义

第二段：分析图表出现这种趋势的原因

第三段：总结，自己的观点，前景预测等。

首段

  1. As can be seen from the chart/ table, it displays the changes of…from …to..

    Especially, from xx to xx has witnessed dramatic changes in… While … It is obvious from the table that…

    正如图表/表格中所见，他展示了从。。到。。时间的什么的变化。特别的是，在xx到xx时间有了引人注目的变化 ，同时….. 从图表明显可以知道+内涵

中间段

  1. There are numerous reasons accounting for the changes, and I would like to explore a few of the most important ones here. To begin with…..In the second place……The last but not least……

尾段

  1. From what has been discussed above, we may finally draw the conclusion that + 总结

  2. Moreover, it seems that this trend will last in the foreseeable future.

    另外，看起来这种趋势会在可预见的未来持续。

例文

How People Spent Their Holidays

Year 1990 1995 2000
Traveling 37% 51% 76%
Staying at home 63% 49% 24%

As can be seen from the table, it displays the changes of ways of people spent their holidays. Especially, from 1995 to 2000 has witnessed dramatic changes. In 1990, 63% of people spent their holidays at home, while the figure dropped considerably to 24% in 2000. It is obvious from the table that people tended to be more and more mobile, and they wanted to get entertainment in the outside world.

　　There are numerous reasons accounting for the changes, and I would like to explore a few of the most important ones here. To begin with, with the rapid development of economy, people earn by far more money than they used to. Thus, people are able to afford traveling expenses. In the second place, people think it important to get well-informed about the outside world in this new era when everything is on the move. Traveling, of course, is a sure way to achieve this goal. What’s more, in this competitive society, people are usually under great stress. However, it is not difficult to find traveling an effective way to relieve people’s stress and get relaxation. Last but not least, by traveling outside, people are close to nature, which is not only beneficial to both their health and peace of mind.

From what has been discussed above, we may finally draw the conclusion that people prefer to go traveling rather than stay at home during holidays for a combination of reasons. Moreover, it seems that this trend will last in the foreseeable future.

英语学习---作文模板（名言类）

发表于 2019-03-15 | 分类于 英语

名言类作文写作思路：

第一段：引出谚语，谚语的内涵

第二段：具体举例说明（或者说xx的重要性）

第三段：总结, 说一下自己对这句名言的看法，以及呼吁人们应该学习这句名言。

首段

  1. It is often said that+ 名言. What the saying is trying to indicate is that+ 内涵。Indeed, we can learn many things from it.

    有一句名言经常被说起….. 这句名言表明的是…… 实际上，我们可以从这句话学到很多东西。

中间段

  1. 两种写法：

    • 一种名言是可以扯到重要性的：就列举它的重要性 There are numerous factors, The first and most important reason is that…There is another factor that deserves some words here,…. Last but not least…..

    • 平常的，不能扯到重要性的，就吹一些当今社会与名言相关话，然后说一两个实际例子。Our society abounds with the example of the proverb. + 举例说明

尾段

  1. From what has been discussed above, we may finally draw the conclusion that + 名言对我们的好处。From my point of view, only through +名言具体的精神+can we achieve our potential and live a better life in this rapidly developing society.(呼吁)

例文

题目 “Respect others，and you will be respected.”

It is often said that Respect others，and you will be respected. What the saying is trying to indicate is that if you want to gain esteem from others, you must do the same thing before.

There are numerous details related to the significance of respecting others, and I would like to explore the most important ones here. To begin with, respecting people around you is one of our traditional brilliant cultures that ought to be inherited and maintained forever. Actually, it is a good way for us to spread our custom to the world through our daily behavior, for example, respecting the foreign students when we study together. What is more, this valuable quality is not only a symbol of your politeness but also a useful way to build relationships. To be honest, no one wants to make friends with those who are contemptuous. Last but not least, as long as you respect others, they would in return take the same attitude to you and even appreciate you, which has the ability to place you in a beneficial position in the future.

From what has been discussed above, we may finally draw the conclusion that the respect others plays and important role in our life. From my point of view, only through respect others can we achieve our potential and live a better life in this rapidly developing society. Thus, we are supposed to respect people whenever and wherever.
