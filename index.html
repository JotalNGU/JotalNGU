六级翻译（2）

发表于 2019-06-04 | 分类于 英语

1.在帮助国际社会于2030年前消除极端贫穷过程中，中国正扮演着越来越重要的角色。

China is playing an increasingly important role in helping the international community to eliminate extreme poverty by 2030.

解析：

  1. play an increasingly important role 扮演越来越重要的角色
  2. in helping in后的动词加ing
  3. international community 国际社会
  4. eliminate 消除
  5. poverty 贫穷
  6. extreme 极度的
  7. 在2030之前 ：by 2030

2.自20世纪70年代末实施改革开放以来，中国已经让多达4亿人摆脱了贫困。

Since the late 1970s, having inplemented the reform and opening up policy, China has helped up to four hundred million people out of poverty.

解析：

  1. Since the late 1970s : 20世纪70年代
  2. reform and opening up policy 改革开放
  3. 实施 implement
  4. up to 4000 多达4000
  5. out of poverty 摆脱贫穷

3.中国在减少贫穷方面取得了显著进步，并在促进经济增长方面作出了不懈努力。

China has made remarkable progress in reducing poverty, and has made unremitting efforts to promote economic growth.

解析：

  1. remarkable progress 显著进步

  2. unremitting efforts 不懈努力

六级翻译（1）

发表于 2019-06-02 | 分类于 英语

1.中国的创新正在以前所未有的速度蓬勃发展。

China’s innovation is booming at an unprecedented rate.

解析：

  1. innovation 发展
  2. “蓬勃发展”可以翻译为动词形式， is booming
  3. 以……的速度：at a … rate
  4. unprecedented 前所未有的

2.为了在科学技术上尽快赶超世界发达国家，中国近年来大幅度增加了研究开发资金。

In order to catch up with and even surpass the developed countries in science and technology, China recently has increased the research and development funds greatly.

解析：

  1. 为了达到…目的 In order to…
  2. 追上甚至赶超 catch up with and even surpass
  3. 发达国家 Developed Country
  4. 研究开发资金 research and development funds
  5. “大幅度” 直接用副词 greatly 来表达。

3.他们还与世界各地的科技园合作，使创新成果商业化。

They also cooperate with Science Parks from all corners of the world to commercialize the innovative achievements.

解析：

  1. cooperate with 合住
  2. Science Parks 科技园
  3. from all corners of the world 世界各地的。 还可以翻译为 all around the world.
  4. commercialize 商业化
  5. innovative achievements 创新成果
